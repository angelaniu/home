GamJaTang (Mapo)

4916 Yonge St, North York, ON M2N 5N5

A popular Korean dish which contains a spicy soup/stew made from pork bones. It contains:

  • Potatoes
  • Kimchi radish
  • Perilla seeds and leaves
  • Green onions and peppers
  • Sesame seeds
  • Bits of meat

The Yelp page on GamJaTang has more reviews if you are interested.

Suitable for a group of 1~4 people

Price range: $10 - $15
