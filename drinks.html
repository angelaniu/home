CHATIME

Chatime is a taiwanese originated shop that sells high quality bubble tea in Toronto. Chatime has over 800 locations worldwide and 20 of the locations can be found here in Toronto. Customers have freedom in customizing their drink along with the massive menu that rapidly increases.

Chatime can be great place to talk to your friends, family or just for yourself.
